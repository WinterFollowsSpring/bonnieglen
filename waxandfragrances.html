WAX & FRAGRANCES

We use Golden Brands 464 all-natural soy wax in all of our candles. This wax uses soy based additives that help reduce frosting and strengthen the fragrance throw. We also use Golden Brands 494 in all of our wax melts and freshies. This is a harder all-natural soy wax that allows us to use the maximum fragrance load of 12% to give you the best fragrance throw.
